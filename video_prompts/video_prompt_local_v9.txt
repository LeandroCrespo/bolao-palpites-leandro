=== PROMPTS DE VIDEO — MESTRE LEME (VÉSPERA DE BRASIL x HAITI — BAR PERTO DO ESTÁDIO, FILADÉLFIA) ===
7 clipes de exatamente 8 segundos cada — falas curtas para caber com folga.
Nenhuma imagem é necessária (anexar a foto do Mestre Leme como referência única é opcional).

CONTEXTO: amanhã (19/06) o Brasil enfrenta o Haiti em Filadélfia. Mestre Leme está
num bar de torcedores bem perto do estádio, véspera de jogo, clima de expectativa
e festa. Ele aproveita pra mostrar um cartão-postal turístico bem conhecido da
cidade — a famosa escultura colorida das quatro letras formando a palavra "AMOR"
em inglês (vermelha, azul e verde, com a letra O inclinada) que fica numa praça
pública — e depois dá as notícias do bolão.

DADOS REAIS (usar nas falas): no topo, EMPATE entre Marcia Monfardini e Marcos
Leme, com 225 pontos cada; Renata Alves Leme Inocencio em 3º, com 220. Na zona
de risco (últimas posições): Sandra Alves Leme com 170, Amanda Leme com 165,
Mario com 160, e lanterna isolada Daiana Benedita Alves Leme com 150.

FIXED CHARACTER: Mestre Leme — a stocky, heavy-set Brazilian man, approximately
55 years old, festive and warm, the happy buzz of eve-of-match energy (match the
provided reference photo exactly, if attached).
FACE: expressive full face with warm light-tan skin, FULL tousled dark
salt-and-pepper hair — medium-short, slightly messy, graying at the temples and
sides (NOT shaved, NOT cut close). Short scruffy salt-and-pepper beard of a few
days' growth covering jaw and chin, blending into a fuller graying mustache.
Thick dark eyebrows, warm brown eyes with deep laugh lines, strong nose, a huge
open contagious smile showing the upper teeth.
BODY: stocky, heavy-set build with a proud round belly, broad shoulders,
expressive Brazilian hand gestures.
OUTFIT (IDENTICAL in every clip): bright canary-yellow Brazil football jersey,
fully visible; a green-and-yellow trucker cap pushed slightly back on his head.
In his hand, a cold beer mug with condensation dripping down the glass.
SETTING: a lively sports bar terrace just blocks from the stadium in
Philadelphia, early evening — strings of warm bulb lights overhead, Brazilian
and Haitian flags pinned along the bar counter, other fans in yellow jerseys
laughing and chatting blurred in the background, and through a gap in the
terrace railing, partially visible down the street, the colorful sculpture of
four giant letters spelling out "AMOR" in English (red, blue and green blocks,
the middle letter tilted), lit up as dusk falls.
STYLE: photorealistic, cinematic, shallow depth of field with the bar crowd and
the sculpture softly blurred behind him, warm golden bulb-light color grade.
